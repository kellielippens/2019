Kilo, Tijd, Vergankelijkheid

Clay installation│2017

For an assignment, we went to the exhibition ‘How beautiful it is and how easily it can be broken’ in SMAK Museum for Contemporary Art, Ghent. The work of Emma Van Roey, 25 kilo, 7u 10’ has inspired me to make this work. Converted into clay as material with its own properties.

By giving my work the title: ‘Kilo? Tijd? Tijdelijkheid’ ('Kilo? Time? Temporary'), I refer to the original work.
